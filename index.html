Who we are

Designers who speak both sketch and job‑site.

Think Studio Architecture is a boutique practice based in La Grange, Illinois. We specialize in human‑centered residential additions, adaptive reuse for small businesses, and community‑scale projects that respect neighborhood fabric.

Our drawings are buildable on day one: coordinated with structural systems, energy details, and the actual cost realities of Chicagoland construction. We model every project in 3D to stress‑test light, layout, and life‑safety before a single wall moves.

The studio is intentionally small so principals stay hands‑on—from the first site walk to the punch list. We love solving messy constraints: landmark parcels, tight lots, layered zoning, and impossible basements.

Most of all, we measure success by how you live in the space six months after move‑in: less clutter, more daylight, and a plan that flexes as your family or business grows.

What we do

Services shaped by daylight, data, and permits.

Three core offerings cover the full journey from napkin sketch to final inspection. Each service includes transparent milestone pricing and coordination with the village and trades.

Residential Architecture

We craft additions, gut renovations, and new homes that maximize natural light and storage on Chicago‑area lots. Our plans are detailed to speed permitting in La Grange, Western Springs, and neighboring jurisdictions. Expect energy‑forward envelopes, flexible rooms, and kitchens built for real life.

Adaptive Workplaces

For owners and tenants, we convert underused shells into efficient studios, clinics, and boutique retail. We coordinate structural, MEP, and egress early so build‑outs price accurately. Brand, acoustics, and wayfinding are integrated—not bolted on at the end.

Community & Civic

We help churches, libraries, and non‑profits upgrade welcome areas, accessibility, and multipurpose rooms. Our phased construction strategies keep programs open while work proceeds. Grant‑ready drawings and straightforward narratives make board approvals easier.

How we work

From first coffee to final inspection.

Transparent fees
Weekly check‑ins
Builder‑ready sets

1) Discovery & Fit

We visit the site, listen to needs, and outline constraints. You receive a brief with options, a target budget, and a fixed‑fee proposal. If we’re the right fit, we sign and get you on the calendar.

2) Design & Approvals

We iterate in 3D with live screenshares so decisions are clear. The set is coordinated for structure, energy, and code; then we manage village submittal and responses until the permit is issued.

3) Build & Closeout

We support bidding, answer RFIs, and visit the site at milestones. When work completes, you get updated as‑builts, maintenance notes, and photography if desired.

Helpful wording

Suggested phrases for your inquiry.

Copy any phrase into the form or select it from the dropdown. Clear language helps us respond with the right next steps and a realistic timeline.

  • We’re exploring a two‑story rear addition and want to understand zoning and budget ranges.
  • Our priority is more daylight and storage without expanding the footprint—what options do you see?
  • Can you review an existing basement plan for egress and head height before we commit?
  • We need permit drawings for a kitchen remodel with structural changes this calendar year.
  • Please outline phased construction so we can live in the house while work proceeds.
  • We’re fitting out a 1,800 sq ft clinic—seeking help with egress strategy and plumbing fixture counts.
  • Looking for adaptive reuse options for a brick storefront with an aging structure.
  • Our board needs a concept package for grant submittal—what’s the best path?
  • We’d like code analysis for occupant load and accessibility upgrades at an assembly space.
  • Can you provide a third‑party peer review of a contractor design‑build set?
